Have a say

Giveth's Donations 3.0 allows for donations to be out of the blackbox. Donations are tracked, reviewed and approved through a process of milestones. You have the last word on deciding where your donation goes.

Results Matter

Giveth is built on network on community values of transparency and accountability. Unlike traditional donating, the Decentralized Altruistic Organizations are powered by a Liquid Democracy Delegation Model that incorporates a simple but robust system for delegating power to manage the funding process.
